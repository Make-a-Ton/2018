Electrical Switches in Series

Two Switches in series






Components: Two Switches,One Bulb,Connecting wires


In the circuit we have two switches which are connected in series. In the case where both the switches are turned on the bulb lights up. In the case where both the switches are off or when one of the switches are turned off the bulb does not light up. In the circuit current only flows when both the switches are turned on. Hence in all other cases current does not flow
